Frequently asked questions
What are media hours and AI credits?
Media hours track uploaded media files and media recorded in Descript, regardless of whether they're transcribed (though you still have the option to transcribe them). AI credits track your usage of AI features like Underlord, Studio Sound, Green Screen, Eye Contact, and AI-generated media and avatars.
I’m interested in setting up an Enterprise plan for my business. Where can I learn more?
Contact sales
to learn more about our Enterprise plans.
Do I need to enter a credit card to try Descript?
No, Descript's Free plan includes everything you need to record, edit and mix audio and video content.
How are subscription payments processed?
Descript Hobbyist, Creator, and Business plan subscriptions are processed automatically to the credit card you provide, according to your selected billing cycle. Users requiring custom invoicing options (such as ACH transfer) can reach out to our
Enterprise team
to learn more.
