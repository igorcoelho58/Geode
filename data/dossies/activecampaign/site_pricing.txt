What happens if my contact list grows beyond my current plan’s limit—will I be penalized or locked out of features?
If your contact list grows beyond your current plan’s limit, ActiveCampaign makes it easy to upgrade to the next contact band or plan without penalties or losing access to features. You’ll never be locked out of your account or forced to pay unexpected fees—your investment simply scales as your business and opportunities expand.
Are there any setup costs or hidden fees when starting with ActiveCampaign?
There are no setup costs or hidden fees when you start with ActiveCampaign. Every plan includes core features and support to help you launch quickly and confidently. You can get started on a trial plan right away, and move to a contract.
How does ActiveCampaign help ensure you only pay for what you actually use, rather than bundling in unnecessary extras?
ActiveCampaign’s modular approach means you choose the plan and add-ons that fit your current needs, without being forced into bundles or paying for features you don’t use. As your business grows, you can add channels or advanced capabilities, ensuring your investment always matches your goals and usage.
