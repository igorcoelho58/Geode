FAQ
Frequently asked questions
What Kommo plan should I choose?
The Base plan is ideal if you are looking to unify all your communication channels in a single app and organize your sales process better.
The Advanced plan is a good fit if you want to power up your processes by automating your workflow, sending broadcasts, and upgrading your chats with bots.
If you’re a super user and want to make the most of Kommo’s AI power up for Salesbot, lead scoring, data backup and more, then Enterprise plan is for you!
Can I pay for Kommo monthly?
Monthly payment is not currently available. The minimum subscription period is 6 months.
Are there additional costs?
No, all of your plan’s features are included in its price. The only extra fees you might pay (which are totally optional) are for 3rd party integrations or services that you choose to purchase.
If I cancel my subscription, can I get a refund?
Yes, you can request a refund for the period you haven’t used yet. Simply chat with our customer support team in your account or email us at support@kommo.com.
Which features are available during my trial?
During the 14-day trial period, you get full access to all Kommo’s features. The only restriction is that media storage is limited to 10GB.
Can I add users to my account for free?
You can add both paid and free users. Paid members can move freely through your Kommo account, using any account features that you have given them access to use.
Free users have limited rights: they can view and chat within the client profiles that have been specifically shared with them.
Which plan lets me connect and use messenger apps?
You only need a Base plan to integrate all your messengers and more than 100 other integrations on our marketplace.
What currency can I pay in?
We bill in USD only, but you may be able to find an official partner who resells Kommo in your currency.
What payment methods does Kommo support?

Does everyone on the account need to have the same plan?
Every user on the account needs to be on the same plan.
