Leonardo, a global leader in the Aerospace, Defence and Security sector, develops multi-domain integrated technologies that effectively meet the needs of every mission. Multi-role and net-centric observation, evaluation, command and control, and intervention capabilities are key to ensuring operational superiority in every domain (Air, Land, Sea, Space and Cyber).
