Mission
Making customer experience frictionless
Brands are successful when they develop relationships with the people who trust them. At Tidio, we believe the best way to build relationships is through excellent customer service.

Our Journey
Tidio was founded in 2013 in Szczecin, Poland, by CEO Tytus Gołas. The company has grown to more than 180 employees across offices in San Francisco, Warsaw, and Szczecin, Poland.
Backed by $25M in Series B funding, Tidio continues to expand its offerings to include agentic AI for customer service. More than 300,000 companies worldwide trust Tidio to help them deliver responsive, high-quality customer service.
